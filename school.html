Maze Generator

Depth-first search

The program demonstrates the use of the algorithm Depth-first search with OOP. I used C++ language. At the beginning, the program creates a list of cubes. The cubes are then spread out to show squared paper. The image is in SVG format. In the next step, the algorithm starts going through individual cubes in a random direction. Depending on which direction he goes, the border of the cubes will be destroyed. A maze will be created.
